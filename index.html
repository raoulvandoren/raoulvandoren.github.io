Curiosity is my default setting — but I like my conclusions certified.
My research interests are verification, certification, and automated reasoning, especially where they meet AI or security.
My goal is to bring reliability to safety-critical systems by certifying the software they depend on. As machine intelligence spreads into real-world infrastructure, dependable guarantees matter more than ever. Software systems shouldn’t just be fast or clever. They should be trustworthy, backed by evidence people can point to, not just vibes (even thought good vibes help).
